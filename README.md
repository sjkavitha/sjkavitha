## 🚀 Featured Projects

<details>
<summary><b>🔋 Autonomous Stand-By Battery System in Electric Vehicle</b></summary>
<br/>

Designed and simulated an ESP32-based dual-battery EV system integrating PMG dynamo energy recovery with regenerative braking, aimed at extending real-world driving range without added battery capacity.

| Aspect | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Stack** | ESP32, PMG Dynamo, Embedded C, Circuit Simulation |
| **Energy Recovered** | ~730 Wh via regenerative braking |
| **Range Extension** | +18.25 km over single-battery baseline |
| **Total Range Achieved** | ~118 km (18% improvement over 100 km baseline) |
| **Impact** | Demonstrates viable low-cost energy recovery approach for EV range extension |

This project explored how a secondary standby battery, charged through a PMG dynamo during braking events, could meaningfully extend vehicle range in low-cost EV platforms — an approach relevant to budget and mid-range electric vehicle design in emerging markets.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>💧 Smart Portable Real-Time Detection System for Microplastics in Water Bodies</b></summary>
<br/>

Designed a low-cost, portable microplastic detection system for real-time water quality analysis, avoiding chemical staining through dual-mode optical sensing.

| Aspect | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Stack** | Dual-mode optical sensing (imaging + light scattering), Image Processing, Lightweight ML |
| **Detection Range** | 100–300 µm particle sizing |
| **Method** | Chemical-free, real-time analysis |
| **Impact** | Enables field-deployable water quality monitoring at low cost |

The system combines imaging and light-scattering sensing modes to both detect and size microplastic particles on-site, targeting affordability and portability for environmental monitoring applications.

</details>
